Bundled athan recording — source and license.

The single bundled recording below is Fajr-specific (includes the
line "as-salatu khayrun min an-nawm" — prayer is better than sleep —
recited twice after the shahadahs, which only appears in the Fajr
adhan). Shipped so the app has a day-one playable athan before the
user imports their own.

    doha_fajr.mp3    Fajr adhan — Public Domain 1.0
      Source: https://archive.org/details/adhan.recordings.from.doha.qatar
      Recorded in Doha, Qatar (2013–2014) and placed in the public
      domain by the uploader via CC Public Domain Mark 1.0.

Users add more Fajr recordings via the "Add audio file" button in
the Prayer tab. The app plays whichever is selected (or a random
one from the available pool) only at Fajr time.
