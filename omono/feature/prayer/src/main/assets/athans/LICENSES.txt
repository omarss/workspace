User-curated Fajr athan recordings.

The recordings in this directory were sourced and provided by the
app's owner (omar.s.shaaban@gmail.com) for personal use on their
own device. The repository owner takes responsibility for ensuring
the recordings are licensed for their personal sideloaded use of
the app; nothing in this directory is curated or asserted-clean by
omono's automated tooling.

If you fork omono and intend to redistribute, replace the contents
of this directory with files you have the right to redistribute,
or remove them entirely (the app falls back to the system default
alarm sound when the directory is empty).
